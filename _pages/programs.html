Education

Hacker Hours

Weekly workshops led by students, alumni, professors, and industry representatives on a variety of technological topics not covered in CS classes, such as languages/frameworks and other topics in theoretical or applied computer science. All Hacker Hour videos, slides, and materials are available on the USACS YouTube channel.

Mentorship

1:1 mentorship matching underclassmen with upperclassmen to provide students with holistic mentoring on academic success, career and personal development, and technical knowledge. 100+ mentees enrolled in the program.

Paired Programming

Paired Programming is a great way to improve your coding skills and learn specific concepts regarding algorithms, data structures, and other computer science concepts. In Paired Programming sessions, one of the session leaders will explain a certain CS topic (or usually a LeetCode problem) and encourage attendees to discuss and, if it is a problem, solve and analyze it. Paired Programming is most useful for interview prep and refreshing your Data Structures knowledge!

Tech

UHACCS

UHACCS is an annual hackathon held by USACS, where students are encouraged to work togather in teams to create projects. The projects are then given feedback by the judges at the end of the event. UHACCS is great practice for students who want to learn new technologies, improve their teamwork skills, and have fun building applications!

USACS Labs

USACS Labs is a USACS initiative where students meet every 1-2 weeks and work on projects while recieving guidance and advice from a USACS board member and other attendees. Students develop their own idea for a project, decide which technologies to utilize, and spend the semester building this project which they can show on their Resume. USACS Labs is an excellent way for students to learn new technologies while bolstering their Resume and meeting other passionate students!

Community

Community Events

Events focused on the social aspect of bringing the community together to make friends and enjoying food and games together! We host events such as Board Game Night, Ladies Sleepover, Tech Involvement Fair, PJ/ Onesie Night, Diversity Social, Bubble Tea Social, USNACCS Snack Tasting, and more! We also collaborate with other clubs (WiCS, Fizzbuzz, COGS, SHE, RUMAD, etc. and take suggestions for events :)

Outreach

Company & Speakers

Manages the USACS Speaker Series program. In the past, we’ve had professors, data scientists, independent developers, and entrepreneurs give talks at Rutgers. We also host and manage trips to companies in NYC and NJ, including Google and Facebook.
