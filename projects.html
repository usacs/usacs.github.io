Projects made by the community!

We do fun stuff. We make fun stuff. Check out some of our best work!




Prank-Roulette

A web app that allows users to set up phone calls between two people and enables the user to anonymously listen.

Shrednought

Shrednought takes in audio created from a real guitar and is relayed as controls for a 3D world survival game.

iSight

iSight uses computer vision via tesseract to convert any image file to braille to assist the visually impared.

Transactr

Transactr tracks your spending habits by updating you through your pebble watch about your recent transactions.

SmartFindr

Helps people quickly browse webpages by targeting the important concepts within a dense amount of material.

Wikilio

Uses Twilio to enable non-smartphone users to research and browse wikipedia through text/image messages.
